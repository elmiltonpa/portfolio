03 Philosophy

We believe in the power of uncomfortable clarity.

Modern design has become too soft, too rounded, and too predictable. Our practice is an antidote to the "friendly" internet. We build digital spaces that demand to be seen, using the principles of Swiss minimalism and Brutalist architecture to create permanence in a world of ephemeral content.
